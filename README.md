# ScratchToTopNotch 🎓🇩🇪

**A free, step-by-step chatbot for Indian students applying to German universities.**

No consultancy. No confusion. Just a clear guided journey from scratch to top notch.

## 🌐 Live Demo
👉 [scratchtotopnotch.netlify.app](https://scratchtotopnotch.netlify.app)

---

## 💡 Why I Built This

Most Indian students who want to study in Germany have no idea where to start. They don't know what APS is, why a passport is needed before booking IELTS, what uni-assist does, how to shortlist universities, what documents to prepare, when deadlines fall, or how the visa process works.

Because of this confusion, students either:
- Pay ₹50,000–₹2,00,000 to consultancies — and still don't understand the process themselves
- Apply randomly — miss deadlines, submit wrong documents, send the same SOP everywhere, and get rejected
- Give up entirely — thinking it's too complicated

**This chatbot clears all of that.**

It walks every student through the entire process — step by step, in order, with disclaimers where things can go wrong — so they actually understand what they are doing and why. From choosing a degree to getting a visa, every step is explained in plain language.

The goal is simple — a student who completes this chatbot should be able to sit down and start their Germany application on their own, without paying anyone, without getting confused, and without missing anything important.

## ✨ Features

- 🎓 Personalised Bachelor's and Master's checklists
- 📘 Language requirements — IELTS, TOEFL, TestDaF, DSH with prep guides
- 📄 APS certificate guidance — documents, cost, timeline
- 🌐 Uni-assist account setup — step by step
- 🎯 University shortlisting strategy — DAAD, 5–7 universities
- 📁 Document uploads checklist + file formatting rules
- 📅 Deadline calculator — Winter and Summer semester
- 🛂 Visa preparation — blocked account, embassy, process
- 📝 SOP structure — Bachelor's and Master's paragraph by paragraph
- ✉️ LOR guide — who to ask, what to include
